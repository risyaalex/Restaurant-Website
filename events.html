Events & Specials

August 20th - Wine Tasting Event

On August 20th, we are thrilled to invite you to our exclusive Wine Tasting Event at Malmö Restaurant.

Tickets for Wine Tasting Event are limited, so we encourage you to reserve your spot early.

More

Treat yourself to an evening of exquisite tastes and aromas as we present a tasting of fine French wines. From the delicate notes of fresh Chablis to the intensity of strong Bordeaux, this event promises to please everyone without exception.

Each guest will receive a free bottle of one of the French wines presented as a memento of this unforgettable evening. In addition, we offer an exclusive discount on all purchases made during the event. Enrich your collection of wines with the best specimens!

Event Details:
Date: August 20, 2023
Time: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Location: Malmö Restaurant
Dress Code: Smart Casual

August 30th - Live Music Jazz Party

Get ready to swing to the soulful tunes and immerse yourself in the enchanting world of jazz at Malmö Restaurant's Live Music Jazz Party on August 30th. This musical extravaganza promises to be an unforgettable night filled with rhythm, melodies, and the charm of jazz.

More

As a special highlight of the Live Music Jazz Party, we are pleased to present an exclusive performance by a jazz band from the USA. So gather your friends and loved ones and join us for an evening filled with the joyful spirit of jazz.

Book your seat at the live jazz party on August 30th and witness the magic of jazz come to life in Malmö Restaurant.

Event Details:
Date: August 30, 2023
Time: 7:00 PM onwards
Location: Malmö Restaurant
Dress Code: Smart Casual

September 10th - Gourmet Party Extravaganza

On this special night, our restaurant will transform into a food lover's paradise, where every dish is a masterpiece. Our team of passionate chefs will present a meticulously curated menu featuring a delightful array of gourmet delights, each prepared with the utmost care and creativity.
